Dark Green Racing

Navigate the road, collect proofs, and avoid obstacles!

Game Overview

Welcome to Dark Green Racing, an exciting driving game where you control a car on a dangerous road. Your goal is to collect as many proof items as possible while avoiding obstacles that will end your run.

How to Play

Use the left and right arrow keys to control your car. Collect proof items (worth 2 points each) and avoid obstacles. The game ends when you hit an obstacle, and your score is saved.

Unlock New Cars

Start with one car and unlock additional cars as you earn points. Each car has its own unique appearance and characteristics.

Environment

Drive carefully! The road is surrounded by water, so stay on track to avoid falling in.
